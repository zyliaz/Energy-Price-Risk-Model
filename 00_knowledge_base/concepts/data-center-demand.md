# Data-Center Demand

Rapidly growing, large, and relatively weather-insensitive load from data centers (AI/cloud)
interconnecting in ERCOT. A structural shift in [[load-and-demand]]: it raises baseline
levels and can change the shape/variability of net load, with potential to alter
[[price-volatility]] and scarcity frequency.

## Status
Hypothesis-stage in this project. Needs sources (ERCOT large-load interconnection reports,
PUCT filings, news) and a way to separate data-center load from weather-driven load.

## Open questions
- Is data-center load measurably raising baseline volatility yet, or just the level?
- How does flat, high-utilization load interact with wind ramps and adders?
