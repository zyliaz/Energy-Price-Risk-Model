# Scope & History

This project's framing has evolved.

- **Original:** comparative study of ERCOT (energy-only) vs PJM (capacity market) —
  how market structure mediates demand/supply shocks → price volatility. (See old repo
  `RESEARCH-PJM-ERCOT-Price-Volatility`.)
- **Current (2026-06):** narrowed to **ERCOT-only**, focused on *decomposing the drivers*
  of price volatility (load, wind, gas, weather, ORDC adders). PJM is background context.

## Why it matters for the wiki
- Do **not** create new PJM-primary pages unless scope re-expands.
- Legacy PJM-comparison material should be filed as background, clearly marked.
- The thesis in [[00_overview]] reflects the current (ERCOT-only) scope.
