# PUCT (Public Utility Commission of Texas)

Texas regulator overseeing ERCOT. Sets key market-design parameters that directly affect
[[price-volatility]] — notably the **system-wide offer cap (SWOC / VOLL ≈ $5,000/MWh
post-2024 reforms)** and the shape/parameters of the **ORDC**.

## Why it matters here
PUCT decisions on offer caps and ORDC parameters bound how large [[ordc-price-adders]]
can get, so regulatory changes are structural breakpoints in any volatility time series.
Track relevant rule changes as sources and note the effective dates.
