# Waha Hub

West Texas natural-gas trading hub near the Permian Basin. Because gas units are
frequently the marginal generator in ERCOT, **Waha spot prices** are a key input to the
marginal cost of power and thus a fundamental driver of [[price-volatility]].

## Notes
- Waha can show extreme (even negative) prices from pipeline takeaway constraints —
  these are worth isolating as outliers when correlating with power prices.
- Data via [[eia]].
